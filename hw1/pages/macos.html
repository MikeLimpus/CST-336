macOS

macOS is a Unix based proprietery operating system developed by Apple. It distinguishes itself from the other operating systems listed on this site in that it is only available on computers made by Apple, such as the Macbook or the Mac Pro. It is the second most popular operating system, behind Windows. [1] One feature that distinguishes macOS is the integration of the OS with the firmware and hardware. macOS is also suffers from fewer cybersecurity threats than Windows [2]
